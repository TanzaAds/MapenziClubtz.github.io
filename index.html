Machame Route

7 Days | 6 Nights Tour Overview Embark on an exhilarating 6-day journey to the summit of Mount Kilimanjaro via the Machame Route, commonly known as the "Whiskey Route." Renowned for its stunning scenery and diverse landscapes, this route presents a challenging yet highly rewarding adventure for trekkers. Beginning in the lush rainforest at the base, the Machame Route traverses multiple climatic zones, offering breathtaking views and unique ecosystems along the way. With gradual acclimatization and expert guidance, this climb enhances your chances of reaching Uhuru Peak, the highest point in Africa.

Marangu Route

Tour Overview Embark on a 6-day journey to the summit of Mount Kilimanjaro via the Marangu Route, the only route with hut accommodations, providing a more comfortable trekking experience. Known as the "Coca-Cola Route," Marangu offers diverse landscapes, from lush rainforests to alpine deserts. This extended itinerary includes an extra acclimatization day, improving your chances of reaching Uhuru Peak, Africa's highest point. With expert guides and a well-structured plan, this adventure is perfect for trekkers seeking a balanced and scenic climb.
